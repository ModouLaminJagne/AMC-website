About

AMC Trading is a food and beverage enterprise that was established in 1984 by 3 brothers: Mr. Abdourahman, Mr. Mamadou and Mr. Cherno with the mission and commitment to serving quality products to enhance better life. To making this mission a reality, we have worked with prominent companies such as Nestle etc. To bring global products to the reach of the masses in all sorts of markets.
We are driven by our values and culture to serve the community and contribute to a healthier future.
